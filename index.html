About

We believe in the principle that no dog in our vicinity has to face rough conditions.
We help rehabilitate strays who are incapable of fending for themselves due to abuse or their health conditions and play an integral part in a stray dog's life.

Our vision is to make a great life for every street dog by providing the shelter in our NGO for dogs.
We want to spread the message of love, peace, and harmony for the welfare of dogs among the animal lovers.
